How Not to Die hits New York Times Best Seller list! NutritionFacts.org Needs Your Help 2015 marks the 4-year anniversary of NutritionFacts.org. In that time, our videos and pages have been viewed more than 50 million times—20 million over the last year alone. Over the last 30 days we’ve reached the milestone of surpassing 2 million hits in a single month. Unfortunately, the rate of new donations has not kept up with the rate of new traffic.
