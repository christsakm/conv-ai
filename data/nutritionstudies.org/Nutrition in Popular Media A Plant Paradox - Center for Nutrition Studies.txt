Nutrition in Popular Media A Plant Paradox - Center for Nutrition Studies In this webinar hosted by eCornell, T. Colin Campbell, PhD and Thomas M. Campbell, MD, bestselling coauthors of The China Study , discuss nutrition science as it is presented in the popular media. They also discuss the recent surge in popularity of the recent popular diet book, The Plant Paradox , by Steven Gundry, MD, which claims that a diverse class of food compounds known as lectins are the main culprit behind much of human disease.
