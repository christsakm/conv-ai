Oil-Free Dressings & Dips - Center for Nutrition Studies Salads and wraps are easy to make and a great way to incorporate a variety of vegetables into your diet. However, they can easily be sabotaged with added oil. Oil-free dressings and dips can provide just as much flavor without including refined sources of fat and dense calories. These dressings and dips also go great over cooked vegetables, cooked whole grains, legumes, baked potatoes, and more!Copyright 2022 Center for Nutrition Studies. All rights reserved.
