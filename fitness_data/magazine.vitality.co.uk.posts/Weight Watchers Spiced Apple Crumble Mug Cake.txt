Weight Watchers Spiced Apple Crumble Mug Cake. This Apple Crumble Mug Cake recipe makes for a tasty snack when you fancy something sweet. SmartPoints values Per serve: 6 Per recipe: 6 10 minutes preparation 3 minutes cooking Serves 1 Ingredients: 1 medium apple, Braeburn, peeled and cut into 2cm pieces 1 teaspoon honey, level 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, level 20g jumbo oats 1 date, dried, chopped 1 teaspoon low fat spread, level Method: Put the apple in a bowl with honey and a pinch of the cinnamon and stir to combine. Spoon into a 200ml capacity microwave-safe mug. In a bowl, combine the oats, date and remaining cinnamon, then rub in the low-fat spread to form a crumble. Top the apple with the crumble, then microwave on high for 2 minutes 30 seconds to 3 minutes, or until the apple is tender.Enjoy!
