Pumpkin Porridge With Blueberries And Mango. Not sure what to do with your leftover pumpkin? Try Safrina Nishad’s delicious pumpkin porridge for breakfast. Ingredients: 1 small culinary pumpkin For the pumpkin porridge: ½ cup quick cooking oats 1 cup milk (or your favourite dairy-free alternative) 1 tsp maple syrup ¼ cup pumpkin puree Toppings of your choice Method: To make the pumpkin puree base Preheat the oven to 190°C, fan 170°C, gas 5. Cut the pumpkin in half and scoop the seeds out and save them for later. Roast the pumpkin halves skin side down for 45 minutes, or until the skin is golden brown and soft. Leave to cool for 15 minutes and scoop out the flesh. Blitz until smooth in a food processor. To make the pumpkin porridge In a microwave safe bowl, mix together oats, milk and maple syrup and cook as per usual (approximately 5 minutes), making sure to stir once in between.
