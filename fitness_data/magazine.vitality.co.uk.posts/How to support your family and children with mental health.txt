How to support your family and children with mental health. Published: 11 May 2022 How do you talk to your children about mental health? Tackling mental health conversations with children isn’t always easy. However, having open and honest chats as a family about how we’re feeling and how to approach those emotions is so important to supporting children with their minds. Vitality’s Head of Mental Health & Wellbeing, Belinda Sidhu, is joined by Priory consultant child and adolescent psychiatrist, Dr Josephine Neal, to explore how parents can better support their children’s and their own mental health.
