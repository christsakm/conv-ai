Weight loss smoothies: Lean Green Machine. Calorie controlled low sugar smoothie at around 170 calories. A perfect breakfast to refresh and energise you for the day ahead. Ingredients: 1-2 cucumbers. handful of kale 5 fresh mint leaves a sprig of fresh parsley. 2 cm pieces fresh ginger. 1/4 avocado. 100ml coconut water. 100ml water juice of 1 lime Add all ingredients to a blender along with a few ice cubes, and blend until smooth. Optional: These additions will increase the essential fatty acid content plus fibre and protein to make it a more filling smoothie but they will also increase the calorie content by around 90kcal per serving if you include both. 1 teaspoon flaxseed oil 1 tablespoon chia seeds
