Weight Watchers Slow Cooked Pork, Bean And Butternut Casserole. Enjoy this delicious winter warming pork casserole, packed with healthy foods. SmartPoints values Per serve: 5 Per recipe: 18 20 minutes preparation 7 hours cooking Serves 4 Ingredients: 4 sprays calorie controlled cooking spray 400g pork loin steak, lean, raw 1 large onion 250g carrots, raw 1 medium red pepper 100g green beans 500g butternut squash 2 cloves garlic 400g tinned tomatoes 480g borlotti beans, cooked 2 tablespoons, level tomato purèe 1 tablespoon dried mixed herbs 300ml fresh chicken stock 1g black pepper 4 sprigs fresh parsley Method: Heat a large non-stick frying pan and mist with calorie-controlled cooking spray. Add the pork, a few chunks at a time, browning them before adding the next batch. Transfer them to a slow cooker. Add all the remaining ingredients to the slow cooker – the chopped vegetables, garlic, tomatoes, beans, stock and dried herbs. Give everything a thorough stir. Put the lid on the slow cooker and turn it on. It will need 5-6 hours on high, or 7-8 hours on low – check your manufacturer’s handbook for recommendations.
