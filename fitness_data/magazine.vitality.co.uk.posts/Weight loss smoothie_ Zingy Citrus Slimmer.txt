Weight loss smoothie: Zingy Citrus Slimmer. Packed with antioxidants and fat fighting ingredients at around 190 calories. Great as a revitalising mid afternoon snack in place of your usual cuppa and some biscuits! Ingredients: 2 handfuls Spinach 1/2 pink or red Grapefruit ½ an Orange handful of frozen raspberries 1 tablespoon Chia Seeds 150ml cooled green tea
