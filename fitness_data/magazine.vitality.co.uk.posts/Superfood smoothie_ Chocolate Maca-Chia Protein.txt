Superfood smoothie: Chocolate Maca-Chia Protein. This protein rich smoothie packed with superfood goodness is great after a workout to aid muscle recovery or makes a good ‘on the go’ energy boosting breakfast that will help keep you full until lunch. Ingredients: 250ml almond, coconut, or hemp milk 1 medium banana (fresh or frozen) 1/2 tablespoon maca powder 125g pea protein powder 1 tablespoon chia seeds 1/2 tablespoon cacao powder 1/2 tablespoon coconut oil Ice cubes Place all the ingredients into your high-speed blender and blend for around 30-45 seconds or until desired consistency is reached. Tip: if you make up this recipe and add a little maple syrup, pop it in the freezer for a few hours to make a delicious ice cream alternative.
