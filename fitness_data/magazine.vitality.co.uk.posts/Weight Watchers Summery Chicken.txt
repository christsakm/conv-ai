Weight Watchers Summery Chicken. A perfect meal for a summer’s day. If you’re dining solo, this summery chicken is easy to prepare and tastes divine! SmartPoints values Per serve: 4 Per recipe: 4 10 minutes preparation 25 minutes cooking Serves 1 Ingredients: 4 sprays calorie controlled cooking spray 150g chicken breast, skinless, raw 100g cherry tomatoes, halved 25g half fat crème frâiche 2⁄3 tablespoon Sainsbury’s classic italian pesto 4 fresh basil leaves, to serve Method: Mist a nonstick frying pan with cooking spray. Season the chicken breast and add to the pan. Cook over a medium heat, turning frequently for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and golden. Add the cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook for a further 2-3 minutes. Add the creme fraiche and pesto, season to taste, stir to combine and squeeze the lemon over.
