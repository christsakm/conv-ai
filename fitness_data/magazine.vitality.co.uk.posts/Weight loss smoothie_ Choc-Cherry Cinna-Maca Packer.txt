Weight loss smoothie: Choc-Cherry Cinna-Maca Packer. A great ‘on the go’ breakfast at around 240 calories that will keep you flying until lunch! Delicious and creamy, plus full of metabolic boosting ingredients. Ingredients: ½ banana Handful of frozen cherries 100ml unsweetened almond milk 100ml water 1 tablespoon sunflower seeds 1 tablespoon raw cacao powder 1 tablespoon maca powder Pinch of chilli powder (or to taste if you want to pack a real punch!) 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
