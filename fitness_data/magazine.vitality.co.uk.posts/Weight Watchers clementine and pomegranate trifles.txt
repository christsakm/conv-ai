Weight Watchers clementine and pomegranate trifles. This trifle is full of deliciously festive flavours and looks gorgeous served in individual glasses. SmartPoints values Per serve: 7 Per recipe: 28 10 minutes preparation 0 minutes cooking Serves 4 Ingredients: 3 slices Weight Watchers carrot cake slices, each cut into quarters 3 medium clementines, peeled, segmented and halved Juice of 1 medium clementine 25ml Cointreau Orange Liqueur, optional 100g 0% fat natural Greek yogurt 2 teaspoons icing sugar 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 100g half fat crème frâiche 50g pomegranate, seeds 10g dark chocolate, shavings Method: Divide the pieces of carrot cake between 4 glasses or bowls and top with the clementine segments. Mix together the clementine juice and Cointreau, if using, and pour over the cake slices and fruit. In a small bowl, mix together the yogurt, icing sugar and vanilla extract. Fold through the crème frâiche and then spoon over the desserts. Scatter over the pomegranate seeds and chocolate shavings and chill until ready to serve.
