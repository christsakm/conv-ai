Pepitas: Toasted Pumpkins Seeds. Want to stop food waste? Food blogger Safrina Nishad recommends saving your Halloween pumpkin seeds to make ‘pepitas’ for a scrumptious salad topping. Ingredients 1 cup leftover pumpkin seeds 1 tbsp olive oil 2 tbsp maple syrup 1 tsp smoked paprika ¼ tsp salt Method To make the toasted pepitas Preheat oven to 150°C, fan 130°C, gas 2 Wash the pumpkin seeds thoroughly and remove any pumpkin pulp that is sticking to it. Wipe the seeds using a kitchen towel or let them dry overnight in the kitchen counter. In a bowl, mix together the pumpkin seeds, olive oil, maple syrup and smoked paprika. Bake in a parchment lined baking tray for 25 – 30 minutes, stirring once half way through. Store in an airtight container and sprinkle over salads, dips and soups or enjoy as a tasty snack.
