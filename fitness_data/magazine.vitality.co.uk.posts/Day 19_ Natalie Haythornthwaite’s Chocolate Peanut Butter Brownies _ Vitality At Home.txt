Day 19: Natalie Haythornthwaite’s Chocolate Peanut Butter Brownies | Vitality At Home. Published on 17 April 2020 Chocolate peanut butter brownies anyone? Yes, please! Vitality Roses England captain Natalie Haythornthwaite has recreated a Joe Wickes classic for us. #StayHealthyAtHome and cosy up with these delicious, gooey delights. Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 12 minutes Total time: 22 minutes Makes 16 brownies Ingredients 140g dark chocolate, chopped into chunks 60g smooth peanut butter 20g runny honey 2 ripe bananas 30g cocoa powder 30ml almond milk 3 eggs, separated into yolk and whites Instructions Preheat the oven to 160C. Heat a large saucepan of water. Put your dark chocolate, peanut butter honey in a bowl over the saucepan and stir until melted. In another bowl, mash the banana until smooth and stir in the milk an egg yolks. Add in the melted chocolate mixture. Whisk the egg whites, and then fold gently into the mixture. Pour the batter into a brownie tray and bake for 12-14 minutes. Remove from the oven, allow to cool and then enjoy!
